## Full description

Copy the block below (no bullet list of other companies’ product names):

> Replace your new tab with a peaceful animated scene: grass, stars at night, and gentle creatures that respond when you move the pointer.
>
> **What you get**
> • Dark mode: glowing fireflies in the field. Light mode: butterflies.  
> • Optional search box and a shortcuts menu in the corner.  
> • Optional strip of your most-visited sites from Chrome, with site icons.  
> • Toggles let you show or hide the search area and the most-visited strip; choices are saved on your device only.
>
> **Permissions**  
> Tabs: used only to open links from the shortcuts menu and the most-visited strip in new tabs.  
> Top sites: used only to read Chrome’s built-in list of frequently visited pages so that strip can show your shortcuts. Nothing is sent to us—we don’t run servers for this extension.
>
> Open source: see the linked repository for code and privacy details.
